# VISION

## Purpose
Production Hub is the operational and analytical backbone of Mintic Design Studio.
It replaces manual spreadsheets with a structured, queryable system for inventory,
sales, and performance data.

## Business Goal
Support Mintic Design Studio in reaching USD 2,000/month by end of 2026
through data-driven decisions on product multiplication, niche expansion, and
inventory management.

## Design Principles
- Data accuracy over speed
- Idempotent operations (safe to re-run)
- Simple, sustainable architecture (one developer, limited hours)
- Light neutral UI, no emojis
- Reports inform decisions — they do not replace judgment

## Non-Goals
- Not a content management system
- Not a Pinterest scheduler
- Not a customer-facing tool
- Not a real-time system
